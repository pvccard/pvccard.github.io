We do not collect or store your Aadhaar Number, Enrollment ID, or OTPs.

3. Log Files

uidaiadharcard.com follows a standard procedure of using log files. The information collected includes:

These are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. The purpose is for analyzing trends and administering the site.

These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.
